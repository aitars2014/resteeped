## Notes for Scraping

All Shopify stores support pagination:
- `?limit=250` for max products per page
- `?page=2` for additional pages
- Products include: title, description (body_html), tags, variants with prices, images

Key fields available:
- `title` - Product name
- `body_html` - Full description with brewing notes
- `product_type` - Tea category
- `tags` - Flavors, origins, caffeine level, etc.
- `variants[].price` - Pricing by size
- `images[].src` - Product images
