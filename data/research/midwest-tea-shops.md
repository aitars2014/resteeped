## Chicago, IL

### Chicago Teahouse ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- **Website:** https://chicagoteahouse.com
- **Shopify:** ✅ Yes (`/products.json` accessible)
- **Products:** ~391 (250 + 141 across pages)
- **Specialty:** Premium loose-leaf teas, wide variety (black, green, oolong, white, rooibos, herbal, fruit)
- **Notes:** 
  - Woman-owned, 15+ years in business
  - Managed by Certified Tea Sommelier
  - Named "Best Tea Shop in Chicago" by Chicago Magazine
  - Focus on organic and fair trade teas
  - Trusted by Art Institute of Chicago, Drake Hotel, Navy Pier

### Spirit Tea ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- **Website:** https://spirittea.co
- **Shopify:** ✅ Yes (`/products.json` accessible)
- **Products:** ~114
- **Specialty:** Farm-direct specialty teas from Asia (China, Taiwan, Vietnam, Japan)
- **Notes:**
  - Chicago-based importer
  - Curates seasonal, rotating collection
  - Direct relationships with origin producers
  - Focus on single-origin, ephemeral harvests
  - Higher-end specialty teas

### TeaLula
- **Website:** https://tealula.com
- **Shopify:** ✅ Yes (`/products.json` accessible)
- **Products:** ~276 (250 + 26 across pages)
- **Location:** Park Ridge, IL (near O'Hare)
- **Specialty:** Full range - white, green, oolong, black, Pu'er, matcha, herbal, rooibos
- **Notes:**
  - Also carries teapots and teaware
  - Focus on sourcing from reputable gardens
  - Emphasis on tea lifestyle and education
