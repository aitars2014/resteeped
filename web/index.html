Built by a tea lover, not a corporation.

Resteeped started because I kept forgetting which teas I liked. I tried notebooks, spreadsheets, the Notes app — nothing stuck. So I built the app I wished existed.

There's no VC funding, no corporate roadmap, no data harvesting. Just one person building a tool for the tea community. Every feature exists because a tea drinker asked for it.

Questions?

Is Resteeped really free?

Yes. The core app is free forever — browse teas, use brew timers, log your collection, write tasting notes. Premium adds extra features like unlimited collections for $2.99/month or $19.99/year.

What teas are in the database?

Over 8,000 teas from 70+ independent tea shops worldwide — loose leaf, matcha, herbal, oolong, pu-erh, and more. We focus on smaller, independent brands and add new teas regularly.

Do you sell my data?

No. We collect zero personal data. Check our App Store listing — it says "Data Not Collected." Your tea preferences are nobody's business but yours.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet, but it's on our roadmap. For now, Resteeped is iOS only.

How is this different from other tea apps?

Resteeped is built for mobile-first tea lovers who care about independent shops. 8,000+ teas from real indie brands, built-in brew timers, and zero data collection. No account required to get started.

Who makes Resteeped?

Just one person who loves tea. No VC funding, no corporate owner. Resteeped is an indie app built for the tea community.
