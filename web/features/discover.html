Not another Amazon search

Searching for tea online usually means Amazon results full of mass-produced tea bags, fake reviews, and stale inventory. Resteeped is different. Every tea in our database comes from an independent tea shop. Real shops, real tea, real quality.

Find exactly what you want

  • By type: Black, green, oolong, white, herbal, pu-erh. Or explore a type you've never tried.
  • By origin: Japanese greens, Chinese oolongs, Indian blacks, South African rooibos. Browse by country or region.
  • By caffeine level: High caffeine for mornings, caffeine-free for evenings, or anything in between.
  • By flavor profile: Sweet, smoky, floral, earthy, fruity. Find teas that match what you're craving.

Independent shops, not big box

Resteeped features teas from over 70 independent tea shops worldwide. These are businesses run by people who care about tea. They source carefully, describe accurately, and offer varieties you won't find at the grocery store.

When you find a tea you like, you can go straight to the shop and buy it. We don't sell tea. We help you find it.
